Welcome to Our Local Community!

Location

Batu Gajah is situated in the western part of Perak, approximately 15 kilometers from Ipoh, the state capital. It is nestled in the Kinta Valley, surrounded by lush greenery, hills, and scenic landscapes.

Events

- Community Fair or Carnival: A festive event featuring food vendors, games, live entertainment, and activities for all ages.

- Farmers Market: A weekly or monthly market where local farmers, artisans, and businesses can sell fresh produce, handmade goods, and locally sourced products.

- Community Service Projects: Organize projects like tree planting, food drives, or volunteer initiatives to give back to the community and make a positive impact.

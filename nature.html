Batu Gajah Nature Attraction




KINTA NATURE PARK

This oasis is a home to many waterbirds and a variety of wildlife species. It supports over 80 species of flora and fauna. The Lake Pucung is the largest pond in the park spread over a vast expanse of 41 hectares.

LUBUK TIMAH RECREATIONAL CENTRE

Located just 15 km from Ipoh City and it is a hidden gem, and its water were once used for tin mining in the early 20th century. This area is suitable for taking fresh air, relaxing and for a picnic during weekends.

TAMAN HERBA NEGERI PERAK

Taman Herba Negeri Perak has a total of 8 herbal gardens. The herbs are clustered according to their properties. Three of these gardens are for heritage herbs two are for herbs with medicinal properties, one for aromatic herbs, one for herbs with cosmetic properties, and one for herbs used in ulam or salads.
